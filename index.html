About Lauren

"Quality is not an act, it is a habit." - Aristotle

I am a final-semester graduate student studying web design and online communication through the University of Florida's Mass Communication: Web Design & Online Communication program. I have spent the last six years cultivating my knack for storytelling -- whether verbally through public relations campaigns, or visually with web and graphic design. Two of my most recent web design projects have been featured on the WDOC program's showcase website. I am passionate about delivering a quality, handmade product that effectively tells the story of your brand.

I am currently located along the sunny Emerald Coast in the Florida Panhandle, but offer my freelance services nation-wide. When not working on design, I compete in equestrian competitions, enjoy writing calligraphy, and participate in my local church.
