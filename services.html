No matter the size, category or budget of your project, I will work with you every step of the way to ensure that your product meets the highest quality standard.

Before we begin....

Each project begins with learning about you, the client! The best way to do this is to fill out the Client Needs Assessment form. Think of this form as an introduction to your project requirements.

You can email your completed form to laurenyockey@gmail.com. Once I have received your email, I will offer to schedule a meeting so we may discuss the project in greater detail.

I will then submit a proposal with a strategic project plan, including an assessment of your current digital footprint. Finally, I'll get straight to work making your project a reality.

Web Design: The Process

Your company's website is often the first impression you give to your customers, so a well planned design is crucial to converting a visitor into a customer.

The first step, as mentioned above, is our initial client assessment meeting that will include a project proposal and agreement.

The second meeting is a brainstorming session to decide the look and feel of your website, as well as to determine branding elements that should be incorporated throughout. During this session, we will create your site map or storyboard sketch. From there, I will create the following deliverables and will solicit continuous feedback to ensure I'm on the right track.

Deliverables

  • Style Tile
  • Mockups and Wireframes
  • Site Prototype
  • User Testing
  • Deployment of Final Site
  • Five Post-Project Revisions
